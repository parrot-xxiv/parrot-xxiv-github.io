About

Hey, I’m Eldren, a web developer who can definitely help you center that div. No, seriously, it’s not as easy as it sounds, but don't worry, I got you.

I spend my days building websites that don’t suck, and my nights wondering why "it works on my machine" never works on any other machine. Classic.

I’m the guy who’ll turn your design mockups into functional websites without making you cry into your coffee. And I’m not just a front-end guy—I've got the backend covered too. Whether it’s setting up servers, working with APIs, or deploying your over-engineered todo list app on Kubernetes (if you’re into that kind of thing), I’ve got you. I specialize in development and deployment.

This profile has no flashy animations, no crazy effects that make you feel like you’re on a roller coaster—just clean, functional websites that get the job done without needing 50 libraries just to change a button color. But if you’re looking for something more complex, check out my other projects, where sophistication meets functionality.

When I’m not coding, you can find me pretending to play guitar, skating until my ankle regrets it, or doing my best to avoid the never-ending list of things I should learn.

If you need a website that’s fast, functional, and actually works—or if you just want to nerd out over the latest tech trends—drop me a message. I promise I won’t send you a "hello world" website unless you ask for it.

Let’s make something amazing—or at least not terrible.
